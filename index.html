FAQ

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where you design an application for a certain amount of time. When the time is up we judge your projects based on creativity, technicality, and originality. The person with the most votes wins a prize.

How long will Eaglehacks be?

12 hours long.

Would I need to be a master at programming?

No, you just have to have a passion in computer science and the patience to learn more. Also don't worry about being inexperienced. We have mentors around EagleHacks to help you out

What should I create?

Using your imagination, you can create anything you want

How will hacks be evaluated?

Your projects will be evaluated based on their real-world application, technical complexity, and visual appeal, weighed in that order.

What should I bring?

A laptop. Student ID. Charger. And any other hardware used in your project
